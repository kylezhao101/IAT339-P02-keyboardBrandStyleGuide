Brand components - Keyboard brand description:

This company builds and ships high quality keyboards to customers with multiple options. Custom built keyboards can be requested in a seperate request form. This brand will feature new and unique case designs, and gives customers the freedom of matching and pairing elements such as the backplate, switch, and keycaps.

This company will be seen as a reliable provider of high build quality artisan keyboards, emphasizing on customizability and self-expression.

This brand is welcoming to both old and new keyboard enthusiasts. Information about specific parts will be given, allowing newcomers to learn.

Branding

Roboto - for headings and leadings
Space Mono - general text

Roboto will be used for headings and leadings. Space mono reflects the structure and organized spacing of keyboard keys. Having a monospaced font will create further contrast between the heading font.

Colour palette

F6F6F6 - White

0C080A - Rich Black

6DD3CE - Medium Turquoise

FE5D9F - Brilliant Rose

with the use of monotone colors of black and white, the turquoise and bright magenta colors create further contrast between headings, leadings, prices, and buttons relative to the rest of the page. Regular text will be either black or white depending on their background. Contrary to many other keyboard brand websites that invlove purely monotone and rounded borders, this branding will appear angular, complementing the structured mono space font and further contrasting the pops of magenta and turquoise highlights. Headings may also use the black and white colors, or the other vibrant pair which contrasts with it's paired imagery. Specifically, vibrant keyboard designs may be paired with a white or black heading to contrast each other, and keyboards with monotone colors will be paired with a vibrant heading.
